Road Type Explanation

Due to performance constraints, this site only shows a subset of the Melbourne map.

  • ■ Historical DB – Base road network (historical_db.geojson).
  • ■ Added – Road that appears in the later year but not in the earlier one.
  • ■ Remained – Road exists in both years.
  • ■ Disappeared – Road existed historically but not in the later year.
